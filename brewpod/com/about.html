Our Story

Brewed with Passion Since 1993

What started as a single roaster in a small Manila apartment has grown into one of the Philippines' most beloved specialty coffee brands. Our founder, Carlos Reyes, believed that great coffee shouldn't be a luxury — it should be an everyday ritual.

We source our beans directly from partner farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, Arabia, and the Philippines. Every bag is roasted in small batches to preserve the unique character of each origin.

Today, Brew Pod serves thousands of coffee lovers every week — both through our cozy cafe and our online store. But our mission remains the same: one perfect cup at a time.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Where are your beans sourced from? +

We partner directly with farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, Arabia, Brazil, and locally in the Benguet region of the Philippines. All farms are certified fair-trade and use sustainable practices.

How fresh are your roasts? +

We roast in small batches every Tuesday and Friday. Online orders ship within 48 hours of roasting to ensure peak freshness. Each bag is stamped with the roast date.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk orders? +

Yes! We supply cafes, offices, hotels, and events. Contact us at wholesale@brewpod.ph for pricing and minimum order quantities.

Can I customize my order? +

Absolutely! We offer custom grind levels (whole bean, coarse, medium, fine, espresso) and can create custom gift sets and private label products for corporate clients.

What is your return/refund policy? +

We offer a full refund or replacement within 7 days if you're not satisfied with your purchase. Just send us a message with your order number and we'll sort it out immediately.
